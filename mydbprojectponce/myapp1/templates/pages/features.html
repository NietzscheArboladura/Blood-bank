Features

The purpose of our project is to provide a safe, secure, and convenient procedure to donate and/or receive blood from compatible users. The website presents a simple and organized interface wherein users can sign up as a donor or a patient to accumulate their specific needs. Our project is in collaboration with dozens of Medical Incorporations to provide secure locations for donation transactions to take place. For a better insight of the aforementioned project, check the photos displayed below as infographics on how Blood Donation works as well as its benefits.

Benefits

Donating blood, especially on a regular basis, can be similar to volunteer work. You give of your time (and your literal blood) to help strangers in need. If you go to specific blood donation location each time, you’ll get to know some of the staff who are also dedicating themselves to the cause of saving lives. Donating blood also has benefits for your emotional and physical health. Some benefits of donating blood include reducing stress, free health check-up, improve your emotional well-being, benefit your physical health, help get rid of negative feelings, and provide a sense of belonging and reduce isolation.

  • Benefits of Donating Blood

  • Reduce Stress
  • Physical Health
  • Free health checkup
  • Get to help others in need
  • Improve your Emotional Well-being
